Performs Affinity Propagation clustering on a point cloud. 

Implements the Affinity Propagation algorithm which\+:
\begin{DoxyEnumerate}
\item Computes pairwise similarities between points
\item Iteratively updates responsibility and availability matrices
\item Identifies exemplars (cluster centers) through message passing
\item Assigns points to their nearest exemplar to form clusters
\end{DoxyEnumerate}


\begin{DoxyParams}[1]{参数}
\mbox{\texttt{ in}}  & {\em input} & Input point cloud to be clustered \\
\hline
\mbox{\texttt{ out}}  & {\em cluster\+\_\+indices} & Vector containing point indices for each cluster \\
\hline
\mbox{\texttt{ in}}  & {\em damping} & Damping factor (0-\/1) to avoid numerical oscillations \\
\hline
\mbox{\texttt{ in}}  & {\em max\+\_\+iter} & Maximum number of iterations for convergence\\
\hline
\end{DoxyParams}
\begin{DoxyNote}{注解}
The algorithm works by\+:
\begin{DoxyEnumerate}
\item Building similarity matrix based on negative squared distances
\item Iteratively updating responsibility and availability matrices
\item Applying damping to avoid oscillations
\item Identifying exemplars where responsibility + availability $>$ 0
\item Assigning points to nearest exemplar to form final clusters 
\end{DoxyEnumerate}
\end{DoxyNote}

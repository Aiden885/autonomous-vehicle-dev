Coordinate system description.

We are assuming the world coordinate frame center is at the camera, the ground plane is at height -h, the X-axis is going right, the Y-axis is going forward, the Z-axis is going up. The camera is looking forward with optical axis in direction of Y-axis, with possible pitch angle (above or below the Y-axis) and yaw angle (left or right). The camera coordinates have the same center as the world, but the Xc-axis goes right, the Yc-axis goes down, and the Zc-axis (optical cxis) goes forward. The uv-plane of the image is such that u is horizontal going right, v is vertical going down. The image coordinates uv are such that the pixels are at half coordinates i.e. first pixel is (.5,.5) ...etc where the top-left point is (0,0) i.e. the tip of the first pixel is (0,0)
